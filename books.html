52 Books in 52 Weeks

Reading has always been a big part of my life. When I was a child, I would remember spending a lot of my free time reading as many books as I can. However, in recent years, I haven't been able to spend as much time reading as I would have liked. To change that, I made my resolution for 2017 to read 52 books in 52 weeks. Below, you can find a gallery of novel covers that shows which books that I've read so far in chronological order for 2017. Feel free to click on the cover to see my personal review of the book that I read.
